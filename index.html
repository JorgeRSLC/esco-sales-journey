The prospect journey, end to end.

Every prospect begins with the same ask. What happens next depends on their answer — and on the signals they send us along the way. Click any stage for detail.

Stage 01 · Origin Initial ask
ESCO asks the prospect to schedule a free evaluation and proposal.
Deep dive →
This is the same opening ask for every prospect, regardless of size or vertical. The response sends them down one of two lanes.
